About Us

Our Mission

We strive to bring the very best experience to our customers. We do this by ensuring our products and produce are of the highest quality, and that we provide a relaxed and friendly environment. We want everyone to enjoy their time here and come back year after year.

Whether you are looking for some fun seasonal activities or just looking to shop, we've got you covered. From hay rides to apple picking, there's always something for everyone.

History

Barry's Berry Farm started with our founder, Barry Molina, and his dream of having his own destination farm. He always loved apple picking and hay rides, and decided to share his passion with others. Now Barry's Berry Farm has multiple products and activities available, with more being added every year.
