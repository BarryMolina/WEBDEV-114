A Man of Many Talents

Timothy O'Donnell is a passionate creator and talented producer of many forms of art. He is a dancer, teacher, photographer, director, and award-winning choreographer. He currently co-directs Milwaukee Ballet's second company in Milwaukee, WI.

Choreography

In 2009, Tim won the Milwaukee Ballets international choreographic competition, Genesis. Since then, he has set many works on Milwaukee Ballet's main company, second company, and school.

Photography

Tim is a professional photographer who takes and edits photos both for Milwaukee Ballet and his own personal and professional development. He is also a Photoshop wizard, and edits photos almost as well as he takes them.

Film

Tim directs and edits many promo films for Milwaukee Ballet, which are both advertising campaigns and works of art all on their own. Check the film page to see snippets of some.
